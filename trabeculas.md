## Case Study 1: Procedural Trabecular Generator (MedTech Pipeline)

### 1. The Problem (Context)
Replicating nature’s geometry, specifically the internal trabecular microarchitecture of human bone, is computationally expensive and nearly impossible to sculpt manually at scale for medical animation pipelines.

### 2. The Solution (Methodology)
Implemented a spatial collapse algorithm to replicate the native Voronoi cellular matrix. This guarantees anatomical density before executing the final volumetric SDF integration into the bone wall.

### 3. Tools & Infrastructure
* **Engine:** Blender 3D
* **Framework:** Geometry Nodes (100% Nodal, Data-Driven)
* **Techniques:** SDF (Signed Distance Fields), Voronoi spatial clustering, Volume-to-Mesh optimization.

### 4. Impact & Metrics (The "Level 5")
* **Time Saved:** Reduced asset creation time from days of manual sculpting to instant procedural generation.
* **Performance:** Infinite scalability and non-destructive iterations without crashing the viewport.
